\section{Decimal to Binary Conversion(1.4.1)}

\Large{\begin{itemize}
\item Continuously divide the decimal number by 2.
\item Keep record of the remainder, either 0 or 1.
\item The sequence of remainders is the binary number required.
\end{itemize}}
